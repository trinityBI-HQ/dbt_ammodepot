## Summary

Added anomaly detection to the existing ML pipeline. Three models (revenue, orders, margin %) train on historical baselines and flag statistical outliers in the last 30 days. Alerts display as a warning banner on Page 1 (Today/Yesterday) of the Sales Dashboard. $0 incremental cost — extends the existing weekly Snowflake Task.

## Lessons Learned

| Lesson | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Training/detection must NOT overlap** | `DETECT_ANOMALIES` requires evaluation timestamps strictly AFTER training data. Use separate views |
| **dbt macros work for ML training** | `run_query` via `call statement()` handles SNOWFLAKE.ML calls. No need for Snowflake stored procedures |
| **Single-series anomaly models are fast** | ~3 min each vs 31 min for 115-series FORECAST. No warehouse scaling needed |
| **Zero anomalies is a valid result** | Means the business is operating within historical norms — the model works |
